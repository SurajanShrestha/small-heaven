About Us

Small Heaven Boarding School scholarship project was established in 2008 . The school has been providing value based education for all its students and preparing them to become a responsible citizens of the country.

Our Mission

Skillful Manpower

Try to create skillful manpower through education who can contribute for the nation in future.

Eradicate Evils

Eradicate social evils from village by raise public awareness through education.

Self Dependency

To make children self depended.

Bright Future

Try to make bright future of children.
